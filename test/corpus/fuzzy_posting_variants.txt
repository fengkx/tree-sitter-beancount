==============================

    * Liabilities:B -100 USD

; All postings with exclamation
2024-01-11 * "All exclamation"
    ! Assets:A 100 USD
    ! Liabilities:B -100 USD

; Mixed flags
2024-01-12 * "Mixed flags"
    * Assets:A 100 USD
    ! Liabilities:B -50 USD
